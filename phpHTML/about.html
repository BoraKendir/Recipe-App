Some insightful information before finding a recipe

  • Please keep that in mind that image detection models can't have 100% accuracies, especially with multiple different type of objects at the same frame.

  • Spices, salts, flour, oils are not considered as primary ingredients, and they are considered as a part of a kitchen. This also means that the recipes can have these ingredients in them.

  • For this version of the app, same ingredients as an input will yield same recipes as a result. Option to randomize results will be added after some user feedback.
